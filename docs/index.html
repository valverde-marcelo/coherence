Why Coherence?

Create a local Ed25519 identity. No usernames, no central registry. Pure cryptography-based identity.

No Central Server

Every node is both client and server. Distribute content directly using WebTorrent protocol.

🔗

DHT Discovery

Find users and resolve pointers through decentralized DHT mutable records (BEP44).

✅

Verifiable Posts

Every post is part of a signed hash chain. Tamper-proof history with author attribution.

👥

Find people through friend-of-a-friend gossip. Your social graph is yours to control.

📦

Chapter-Based Posts

Technical Architecture

🔑 Identity

Uses tweetnacl for Ed25519 key generation and signing. Your public key becomes your permanent, verifiable identity across the network.

📡 Content Distribution

Leverages webtorrent to seed and download content chapters. Posts are batched into chapters to improve swarm performance and reduce torrent fragmentation.

🔍 Discovery

Uses DHT mutable items with BEP44 to publish signed mutable records. Each user publishes under the hash of their public key, enabling decentralized "what's new" updates.

🔗 Integrity

Each post references the previous post hash and is signed by the author's private key. Provides verifiable attribution and tamper evidence without global consensus.
